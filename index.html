Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Rescue Flashlight ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Our application is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).

The application only requires camera permission to control the device flashlight. We do not access, capture, store, or transmit any images, videos, or camera data. The camera permission is solely used to enable flashlight functionality.

We store only local application settings (such as preferred mode, brightness level, and volume settings) on your device. This data is stored locally and never transmitted to us or any third party.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal information to use or process.

The camera permission is used exclusively to control the flashlight feature. No camera data is accessed or processed.

Local application settings are used solely to provide you with a personalized experience within the app.

Data Storage

All application settings are stored locally on your device using secure local storage. This data includes:

  • Preferred flashlight mode (Flashlight, SOS, Emergency)
  • Default brightness level
  • Default volume level
  • Language preference

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted externally. You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the application.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any information with third parties because we do not collect any personal information.

Our application operates entirely offline and does not connect to any external servers or services.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete.

You have full control over:

  • Camera permission: You can grant or revoke camera permission at any time through your device settings.
  • Application data: You can clear all application data by uninstalling the app.
  • App usage: You can stop using the app at any time.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect any locally stored application settings. However, since we do not collect or transmit personal information, there is minimal security risk.

All data is stored locally on your device, and you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, the application is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

情報の使用方法

個人情報を収集しないため、使用または処理する個人情報はありません。

カメラ権限は、フラッシュライト機能を制御するためだけに使用されます。カメラデータにはアクセスまたは処理されません。

ローカルアプリケーション設定は、アプリ内でパーソナライズされた体験を提供するためだけに使用されます。

個人情報を収集しないため、アクセス、変更、または削除する個人データはありません。
